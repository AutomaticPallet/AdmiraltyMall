About Admiralty Mall

The Admiralty Mall is located in the prestigious area of Lekki Phase 1, Lagos, Nigeria. The Development features 33 retail stores with an average size of 30sm per store set across 3 floors and 35 parking spaces. The mall also includes office spaces and a roof top bar on the terrace floor. Other features include communal seating areas, beautiful atrium and imported luxury fittings. ...The development is in close proximity to some of the country's most prominent hotels and recreational establishments such as Lillygate Hotel, Sailors Lounge, Bay lounge and Upbeat Activity Center.Also within the vicinity of Lekki Phase 1 are the Four Points hotel, Hard rock Cafe, Civic events center and Civic towers which are within a 15-minute drive to this unique development.
